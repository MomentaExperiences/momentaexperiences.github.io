City of Palo Alto Community Diwali Celebration

A joyful community Diwali celebration created in partnership with the City of Palo Alto and Barron Park Association, bringing neighbors, families, city officials, and the mayor together for a shared cultural experience. Now hosted two years in a row and counting, the celebration was designed to make Diwali feel welcoming and interactive for guests of every background through music, dance, games, and festive connection.

  • Led interactive music and dance moments that encouraged the full community to participate, move, and celebrate together
  • Designed Diwali-inspired games for kids and adults to help guests mingle, connect, and enjoy the festivities
  • Extended this approach to other community and cultural celebrations, including Indian Independence Day and heritage-focused gatherings
