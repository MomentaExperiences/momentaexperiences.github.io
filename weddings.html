Sangeet Evening

A vibrant Sangeet evening created to blend the soul of Punjabi and Indian folk traditions with the energy of a modern wedding celebration. From dholki-led folk songs for the parents to custom lyrics for the couple and dance-floor favorites for their friends, the experience brought every generation into the celebration and set the tone for a joyful night of music, games, and connection.

  • Created a traditional Sangeet atmosphere with Punjabi and Indian folk songs performed on dholki
  • Personalized the music with creative custom lyrics for the couple and meaningful songs for the families
  • Brought guests of all ages into the celebration through interactive songs, dancing, and Sangeet/Mehendi-style games

Custom Choreography & Dance Performance

A polished choreography experience designed to help families, friends, and wedding parties create a memorable performance moment with confidence. From song selection and movement direction to guest-friendly coaching, the experience brings energy, elegance, and personal storytelling to the celebration.

  • Created custom choreography tailored to the group, music, comfort level, and event style
  • Guided performers through approachable steps, formations, transitions, and stage presence
  • Designed a performance moment that can scale for Sangeets, weddings, milestone celebrations, fundraisers, and corporate events
