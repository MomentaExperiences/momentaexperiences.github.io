Hi, I’m Gurleen

My personal mojo in life has always been connecting with people and creating value — whether that’s through my work, my son’s school community, teaching fitness, or bringing a new project to life. I get my energy from people: working with them, learning their story, understanding what matters to them, and creating moments that feel meaningful.

Momenta Experiences is a natural extension of that energy. It gives me a way to combine creativity, movement, music, culture, and thoughtful planning to design experiences that feel deeply personal. My approach is never one-size-fits-all — it’s about creating something that feels so you, so that both you and your guests can feel the intention behind every detail.

Whether I’m helping shape a celebration, leading an interactive experience, or working one-on-one with a client, my goal is always to help people feel confident, connected, and fully present in the moment. Through it all, I bring warmth, excitement, and care to every interaction — so the experience feels truly memorable, because Momenta Experiences is designed around the feeling people remember long after the event ends.
